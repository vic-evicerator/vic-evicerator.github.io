Band History

It started with New World Blasphemy.

In early 2001 Vic of Rampage was organizing a black metal split release for Unsung Heroes Records that featured Rampage, Cross Sodomy, and Song of Melkor. In the course of that, Vic and Dementor decided to form some kind of new project with Dementor handling lyrics and vocals and Vic handling the music. When Vic saw the lyrics, the unremitting violent blasphemy gave him the same feelings as some of the great death/thrash classics of the past and inspired him to pitch the idea of this band being a real thrash band - the kind that had been extinct for 15 years ever since political awareness, moralistic bullshit, and socialist whining devolved one of the most violent forms of music to pathetically laughable 'CNN metal'.

Vic and Dementor created the band name DEATH BEAST and took the names Evicerator and Ironfist. Evicerator began recruiting members and quickly found fellow guitarist Axecutioner. Evicerator and Axecutioner started writing, and within a couple of months four songs and a couple of covers were somewhat finished. Ironfist completed some vocals, and a few solos were still to be done, when Black Goat of BARBARIAN WRATH became aware of the band. He demanded mp3s, so Vic sent him the mostly-but-not-totally finished tracks as a demo, and Black Goat immediately signed the band.

Despite not having a full lineup, the band began fleshing out the demo to full-album length, finishing the unfinished tracks and writing more new ones. By early 2002 a primitive version of The Wakening was completed musically and sent to Ironfist for completion of vocals. Axecutioner had also found a drummer, Black Tormentor, and auditions had turned up a somewhat passable bassist. The band managed to play a couple of shows over the summer of 2002, but then circumstances started conspiring against the band...
