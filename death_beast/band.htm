Notorius Butcherus Poseurii

  • Vocalist
  • Joined band in late 2004 after former vocalist Juggernaut was forced to leave.
  • Originally tried out for guitarist spot in band, was turned down because he couldn't play very well. He did a few vocals during the audition, which impressed Axecutioner and thus got him the call when Juggernaut left.
  • Favorite vocalists: Paul Baloff, King Diamond, Tom Araya (old), Jeff Becerra, Nasty Ronnie
  • Favorite drinks: Jack Daniels, any vodka, any beer, 666 Cold cough syrup
  • No former bands worth mentioning...

Evicerator of Idiotic Losers

  • Bassist/guitarist
  • Formed band with vocalist Ironfist in 2002.
  • Favorite guitarists: Tony Iommi, Glenn Tipton, Gary Holt, Brian Voth
  • Favorite bassists: Cliff Burton, Geezer Butler, Steve Harris, Cronos
  • Vic is also a founding member of Rampage.
  • Uses a Jackson RX-10 Rhoads-style V and a BC Rich Mockingbird (NJ Series)

Axecutioner of the Worthless

  • Guitarist
  • Joined band in mid-2002, just after it was formed.
  • Favorite guitarists: Gary Holt, Mike Torrao, Jeff Hanneman, KK Downing
  • Uses an Ibanez 540R, Jackson Rhoads V, Jackson Dinky Reverse, and Jackson Kelly.

Black Tormentor of Scenester Wussies
