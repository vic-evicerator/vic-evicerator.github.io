Vic

In the not-too-distant past I had some bold words here stating my purpose for the existence of Rampage.

Then, I had some less-bombastic words that proclaimed our purpose - making music that we felt came as our honest artistic expressions to the world.

They still reeked of bombast.

One of the lessons I kept returning to in every literature and writing course I ever took was that, no matter what I thought of what I was doing, the best reactions to anything I created came when I gave up with trying to be something and just expressed some idea with nothing more in mind than making that particular expression in the most honest, direct way.

I'm a metalhead, and I like many different kinds of metal. When I get an idea that I want to express, I'll dive in all the way and do it, hopefully without worrying about anything more than "Is this the target I'm aiming at, and how close am I to hitting it?".
