Rampage tracks taken from Displeasures of the Flesh. and the Ticket to Hell Websingle. Produced by Vic and Andrew Jude Riotto.

Vic's thoughts on UHR Sampler Collection IV:

Aerik gets all the credit for this one, as I was terribly busy at the time this was compiled, so I asked him to give me what his picks would be for the whole sampler if he were compiling it. Well, as it turned out, his picks were so good I just kept them. It also helped that they worked out to just about 42 minutes, leaving just enough time for me to add on that radio interview track at the end.
