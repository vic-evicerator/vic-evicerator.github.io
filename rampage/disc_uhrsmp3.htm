Rampage track taken from Monolith to an Abandoned Past. Produced by Lord Vic.

Vic's thoughts on UHR Sampler Collection III:

Again, I just took what I thought was the strongest track from the releases that fell during the timeframe in question. In this case it happened to be "Doomsayer", one of the oldest Rampage songs and the last one to get a full-band treatment (the riff of this one and basic lyrics were written back in 1993, but this song didn't get 'fully' done until Monolith in 2001). I'm not exactly 'unhappy' with Doomsayer - I like it quite a bit, actually - but it amazes me how many people think it's the best song I've ever written. Ah well, that's a question for art theoreticians and philosophers - I'll just be glad someone headbangs to something I write.
