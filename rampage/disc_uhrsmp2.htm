Rampage tracks taken from Apocalyptic Raids 2001 CE, Bellum Infinitum, and Cummin' Atcha LIVE. Produced by Lord Vic.

Vic's thoughts on UHR Sampler Collection II:

As with UHR Sampler I, the Rampage tracks featured here were simply taken from all of the Rampage albums released during the timeframe in question. The only exception was the New World Blasphemy split - I didn't include either of those tracks on this sampler. However, those two tracks were already available for free online and Rampage was already featured with three songs, so I felt it best to give more time to other UHR bands.
