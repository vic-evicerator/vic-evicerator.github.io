About this album

We received this album completely out of the blue, completely unsolicited. The entity (entities?) calling themselves Undimensional merely sent us this recording and a statement of purpose, which you can read here. Now you know as much as we do. And the music is ambient, celestial, dark, raging black and doom metal.

The Daemons are, The Daemons were, and The Daemons shall be again. They came, and we are here; they sleep, and we watch for them. They shall sleep, and we shall die, but we shall return through them. We are their dreams, and they shall awaken.
