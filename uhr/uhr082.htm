About this album

Our first purely-digital netlabel new release is this live gig from Death Beast. November 2011 marked the first anniversary of the passing of Ramrod Ryan Weiss, and a gig that Lance and Festering Sore organized ended up turning into a tribute show, capped by Lance convincing Evicerator and the gang to do a Death Beast show. A car accident later that night destroyed the video and nearly all of the audio, but a stroke of luck and months of reconstruction have allowed us to piece together this live album. The album can be downloaded from The Bleeding Edge. Thanks to licensing restrictions we couldn't post the full coversong tracks, but you know how you can get them...
