News: November 2023

BACK FROM THE DEAD!

Yes, UHR is resurrected. Our website is still undergoing redesign and fleshing out of back catalogs, but we have NEW news as well, in the form of new releases - both a totally new release and a crucial, long-awaited reissue.

DISJECTA MEMBRA has clawed its way back to life, and so with our recent resurrection we are thrilled to announce a NEW EP from the gang, Back From the Dead! Four tracks, over 25 minutes of newly-recorded classic songs from Disjecta Membra, featuring original guitarist/singer/songwriter Andrew Hudson and new guitarist/bassist/drum-conductor Mark Vignati.

And as if a new release wasn't enough, we have secured the rights to provide a full, proper digital reissue of the band's classic Live at WREK gig in its original, first-generation-from-DAT glory, sounding as pristine as the night it was recorded over 3 decades ago. Check them both out at our Bandcamp page!

We've also fleshed out our catalog page, adding the new releases and some available older ones. The full resurrection of the old catalog, including out-of-print releases, as well as a more detailed history of the label, are also coming soon.
