About this album

Raiding the vault, we have audio documentary of all of Death Beast's former vocalists, from founder Irönfist's throat shredding on the Apocalypse Metal demo to Juggernaut's rehearsal growling to Evicerator's, um, unique audition guide vocal contributions. The covers are a hoot as well, and the liner notes feature a somewhat-complete band history and quite a few fun tidbits.

This album (minus the coversongs) can be found at The Unsung Heroes Records Bandcamp Page.
