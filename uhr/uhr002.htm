Production Notes

About this album

Vic told the tale of the recording from his perspective many years ago:
"Back in 1992 when I was still in Early Warning we had the good fortune to play our first ever live show with fellow-Atlanta headbangers Disjecta Membra. They went on right after us, and ironically it was their first ever live show too! They bowled me over with their opener "Earth and Stone" and as the night went on they pummeled me mercilessly with slab after slab of their own brand of heavy metal.

"Later that month a friend of mine who worked at the college radio station where I was said that Disjecta Membra would be playing LIVE on the radio, and I begged him to get me into the studio. I arrived at about the same time they did and talked with them as they set up, and then I sat enthralled as they played their guts out over the airwaves with me not even five feet away listening. Later that night we hung out and drank beer listening to the tape of that show. I got a copy that night but through various circumstances it disappeared.

"Four years later when I was in Skiptoe we were invited to play on Live at WREK, and while setting up I started talking to the soundman about Disjeca Membra and he said "yeah, I've still got that DAT somewhere..." Amazed, I signed my soul away to him to get a copy of it, and when it arrived about three months later I eagerly popped it in - and it was just as powerful as it was in person all those years ago. At that point I decided 'the world just HAS to hear this'... I still don't feel like anyone else has had quite the mix of metal styles that Disjecta Membra had, and that uniqueness has always been a goal of mine. HAIL DISJECTA MEMBRA!

And finally, after 3 decades, UHR has secured permission to provide a full digital reissue of the live recording for your auditory pleasure. Check it out at UHR's Bandcamp Page today!
