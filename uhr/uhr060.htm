About this album

Gomorrah's third album sees them take a more doomy approach, as well as a slightly cleaner one (they used a 4-track instead of a boombox this time). Still raw and blasphemous as fuck. Hail Satan. It was deleted from print in 2006 but is now available on The Bleeding Edge.
