Production Notes

Recorded March-April 1998 at Bloody Leg Studios, Stockbridge GA. Produced by Vic.

About this album

Rampage's first demo EP and UHR's first active-band original release may have both been less than serious, but the musical attitude on display would be foundational to what the label is all about. This EP was deleted from physical print in 2004, but is now available on The Bleeding Edge. After much thought, this release has been retitled: "Toxic Masculinity".
